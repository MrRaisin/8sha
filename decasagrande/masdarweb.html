INTRODUCTION Masdar is a city in a desert. This city is called the greenest city in the world because it causes no (or nearly no) pollution. Masdar City hosts the headquarters of the International Renewable Energy Agency (IRENA).[6][7] The city is designed to be a hub for cleantech companies. Its first tenant was the Masdar Institute of Science and Technology, which has been operating in the city since it moved into its campus in September 2010. TRANSPORT The city does not use polluting cars, instead they use podcars. Podcars can move around using a magnetic field and they don't need drivers.
