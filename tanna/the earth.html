Facts About The Earth

The Earth has an atmosphere containing 21 percent oxygen and it is the only planet that has life and liquid water on its surface. The Earth is the only planet in our solar system not to be named after a Greek or Roman deity and the only inner planet (Mercury, Venus, Earth and Mars) to have one large satellite, the Moon. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life. The Earth has 1 moon and it's orbit distance is 149,598,262 km, and its orbit period is 365.26 days.
