London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France.It's a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. They are most famous for the building ''The Eiffel Tower.''

They are very famous for lots of food, for example, Escargots de Bourgogne (snails baked in their shells with parsley butter),Bœuf bourguignon (beef stewed in red wine), Coq au vin (chicken braised in red wine, lardons and mushrooms), Eclair au chocolat, Crêpes, Crème brûlée,Baguette,Boule de Pain bread French bread rolls, Butter,Jam, Honey,Croissants,Pain au chocolat.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. Their population is 13.62 million. Tokyo is most famous for its size. Tokyo is also one of the priciest cities and is well known for its rich cultural history.

Famous food in Tokyo is Sushi,Ramen Street,Uoriki Kaisen Sushi,Tenmatsu,Narutomi Soba,Chanko Tomoegata.
